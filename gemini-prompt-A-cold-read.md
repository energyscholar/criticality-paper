# Prompt A — Open-Ended Cold Read

Review the attached paper. Provide a rigorous, structured evaluation. Do not assume the paper is correct. Identify its strongest claims, its weakest claims, what it gets right, what it gets wrong, and what it leaves out. Note any methodological problems, logical gaps, or places where the language outruns the evidence. State your overall assessment and recommendation (publish as-is, minor revision, major revision, reject).
